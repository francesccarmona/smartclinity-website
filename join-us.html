Step 2 · Regulatory

Regulation as a strategic moat.

Medtech regulation is hard. We treat that as an opportunity, not a tax — we built a deployment that snaps onto the workflows hospitals already have.

Step 4 · Finances

Hardware deploys. Software retains.

SmartClinity is not a hardware-only sale. The device opens the door — the recurring SaaS layer is what scales.

Hardware deploys. Software retains.

The device price stays simple and validated. The recurring revenue comes from cloud, AI modules, integrations and B2B licensing — each one independent from hardware unit economics.

62 €
Per device · all-in hardware cost

Cloud platform

Monthly subscription per hospital. Devices, users and clinical units scale the plan. Predictable MRR per validated center.

AI premium modules

Add-ons priced per device, hospital or active patient. AI Insight, Clinical Assist, Enterprise Governance and Home Care — sold separately from the base plan.

B2B licensing

License the platform to robotics, insurance and home-assistance companies. Pricing per patient, device or API — channel scales outside the hospital.
