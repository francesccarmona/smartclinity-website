Business Model

Hardware deploys.
Software retains.
Data compounds.

SmartClinity's business model is built on a hardware entry point, a recurring SaaS revenue layer, and a future AI-powered premium analytics tier — designed for sustainable, long-term clinical value creation.

Model Architecture

A layered model built for long-term clinical scale.

SmartClinity enters hospitals through a hardware deployment — a plug-and-play device that can be installed on existing clinical infrastructure with minimal disruption. Once deployed, the SaaS platform creates a continuous, sticky engagement layer that retains value over time. As data accumulates, the AI intelligence tier unlocks premium analytics opportunities.

"The hardware installs trust. The data creates value. The intelligence builds competitive distance."

Revenue Structure

Three revenue streams. One platform.

Stream 01
Hardware
Device sales or financing arrangements with clinical institutions. One-time revenue with long device lifetimes and natural replacement cycles. Anchors platform adoption.
Stream 02 · Core
SaaS Subscription
Per-device monthly subscription or institution-wide enterprise license. Includes dashboard access, alert configuration, data storage, historical visualisation, and traceability export. Recurring, predictable, scalable.
Stream 03 · Future
AI Premium Tier
Advanced analytics, predictive intelligence, and anomaly detection as an add-on premium layer. Monetises data value after sufficient longitudinal accumulation across the installed base.
